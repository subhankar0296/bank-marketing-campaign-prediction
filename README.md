# Bank Marketing Campaign Prediction using Logistic Regression

## Overview

This Machine Learning project predicts whether a customer will subscribe to a bank term deposit based on demographic, economic, and campaign-related features.

The model helps banks improve marketing effectiveness by identifying customers with a higher probability of conversion.

---

## Business Objective

To optimize marketing campaigns and reduce operational costs by predicting customer subscription behavior.
